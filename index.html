About Me

My name is Daniel Jauregui. I was born in San Jose, California, and when I was just three months old, I moved to a small town called Lathrop, California . I graduated from Lathrop High School in 2019. By the end of my high school career I grew the love of coding and enrolled in UC Berkeley's web development program. I'm a first generation American in my family. I'm the first ever to explore higher education and I find great interest in it as well.

My favorite things to do in my free time coding, playing Rocket League, and watching shows on Netflix. I aspire to work for a large tech company in the bay area. I am also apart of the top 1% in Rocket League.
